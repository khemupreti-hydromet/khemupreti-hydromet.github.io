I am interested in understanding how water and climate interact, especially in complex mountain environments like the Himalayas. My focus lies broadly in hydrometeorology, climate extremes, and climate change, with an emphasis on how these processes connect across atmosphere, land, and society.

I am particularly curious about how hydro-meteorological systems behave under changing climate conditions how precipitation, evapotranspiration, and extreme events evolve, and how these changes translate into real impacts such as floods and water stress.

### Technical Toolkit
* **Data Analysis:** Python, R, and Google Earth Engine (GEE).
* **Modeling:** HEC-HMS and HEC-RAS.
* **Research Focus:** Exreme Climate, Climate Change, ETCCDI climate indices, Hydrometeorological modelling and Remote Sensing.
